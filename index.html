Company Overview

Raven Tech is a cutting-edge IT security company founded in 2024 and headquartered in Malaysia. Specializing in advanced cybersecurity solutions, Raven Tech is dedicated to providing businesses with the tools they need to protect their digital assets and infrastructure. Our flagship products include Extended Detection and Response (XDR), Privileged Access Management (PAM), and Network Management Systems (NMS), each designed to address the unique challenges of modern cybersecurity.

  • Mission

    To empower businesses with innovative and robust cybersecurity solutions that safeguard their digital environments against evolving threats.

  • Vision

    To be a global leader in IT security, known for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer-centric approach.
